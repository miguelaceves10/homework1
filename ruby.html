Ruby


Ruby is an open-sourced, object-oriented scripting language that can be used independently or as part of the Ruby on Rails web framework.It was designed in 1995, Ruby’s creator described it as “simple in appearance, but it is very complex inside, just like our human body.”

Ruby is used for simulations, 3D modeling, and to manage and track information. It is also used for Web App Development, Robotics, Networking, System Administration and Security.

It plays big part in engineering, professional services, design, science and quality control as well.

Fun Fact: NASA uses Ruby to conduct simulations.

Some of the major organizations that use ruby are: Scribd, Groupon, NASA and Motorola.
